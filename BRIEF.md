# Project Brief

## Project Name
ALT Playbook Quiz

## One-liner
An interactive quiz that helps team members learn and retain the ALT (AI-First Lean Teams) Playbook principles.

## The Story
I was going through our ALT Playbook to get up to speed, and reading a Google Doc only goes so far. I thought — what if I could actually test my understanding? So I built a quiz to help me learn it better. Now anyone on the team can use it too.

## Goal
Make the ALT Playbook stick. Turn passive reading into active learning through a short, engaging multiple-choice quiz.

## Audience
- New ALT team members onboarding to the ways of working
- Existing team members who want to revisit or test their knowledge
- Anyone at the company curious about AI-First Lean practices

## Scope
- ~27 multiple choice questions across 7 sections
- No login, no backend, no data stored — just a clean browser experience
- Fully static: HTML, CSS, JS only
- Mobile-friendly

## Success Looks Like
- Someone can complete the quiz in under 10 minutes
- They walk away knowing something they didn't before (or confirming what they did)
- It feels designed — not like a Google Form

## Out of Scope
- User accounts or saved scores
- Leaderboards
- Admin panel for editing questions
- Animations beyond simple transitions

## Design Direction
- Clean, minimal, modern
- Color palette inspired by the AI/tech space — dark mode preferred
- Typography: clear hierarchy, readable at a glance
- Micro-interactions: subtle feedback on correct/incorrect answers
- Progress bar so users know how far they are

## Distribution Plan
Share in the team's design-output channel with the context: "I was learning the ALT Playbook and built this to help it stick — give it a try."

## Built By
A designer at the company using vibe coding as part of learning the ALT Playbook itself — practicing what the playbook preaches.
